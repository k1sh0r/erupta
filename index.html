EVENTS

Students can participate in any number of Events
  • Technical Events
  • Non-Technical-I
  • Non-Technical-II

Paper Presenatation

  • 1 to 3 members per team.
  • 8 min for explanation.
  • 2 min for queries.

Unfold Yourself

  • 2 members per team.
  • Monitor will be turned off.
  • Typo errors will be Neglected.

Project Display

  • 2 to 4 members per team.
  • The model can be working or Non Working.
  • Bring all the accessories to demonstrate.

Debate

  • The topic will be given On-Spot.
  • No offensive languages will be allowed.
  • The decision of the judges will be final.

CAD-Modelling

  • On-the spot Modelling.
  • Duration will be 30 minutes.
  • Questions based on the model and usage of CAD tools will be asked.

Setsuzoku(Connections)

  • Round-1: 2-3 members.Tech gadgets prohibited.
  • Questions will be based on Tech and Non-tech.
  • For each question 30 secs.
  • Round-2:Question will be based on Tech.
  • 15sec for each questions.

Meme Creations

  • Templates can be created on their own.
  • Freestyle memes cannot be accepted.
  • Meme timing 30 min.Countless meme.

Warp Your Style

  • Particular image will be given.
  • Edit the photo in the given time.
  • Topic will be given on-spot

Treasure Hunt

  • Clue booklet will be given, each clue will lead to new location.
  • Team will have to find treasure coin.
  • Evaluation will be based on no.of coins.
  • Round-2:15 min.

Touch and Sense

  • Round1: 1 member ,list of 10 objects,identify the objects inside the room by touching.
  • Many number of objects,identify 5 objects

Love your Lens

  • Take photos within CSE department.
  • Edited Photos are accepted.
  • Time: 1 hour.

Game O Game

  • Will be On-Spot.

IPL Auction

  • Literal IPL auction will take place.
  • Team selecting the best team will be awarded.
